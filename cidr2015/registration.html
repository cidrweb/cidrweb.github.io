Registration

The conference is sold out. We can no longer accept registrations.

  • Before October 27, 2014: early registration for authors of abstracts and submitted papers.
  • After October 27, 2014: registration on first come first serve basis until capacity filled.

Registration is being handled by Asilomar Conference Grounds.

Registration fees are "all-inclusive". They cover room (3-nights), all meals, conference registration and other fees and taxes.
