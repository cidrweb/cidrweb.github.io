Organization
 
This conference is organized by the Conference on Innovative Data Systems Research (CIDR), composed of the 25 people listed below. The plan is to make CIDR a continuing organization with rotating committee members. CIDR will request (but does not require) co-sponsorship from ACM SIGMOD. Funding support for the conference is provided by the individual members of CIDR.
