CIDR Organization & Sponsors

The CIDR conference is organized by volunteers from the data systems research community on behalf of the USA-based 501(C)(3) non-profit organization CIDR, governed by a board of directors and advised by an advisory board, all whose members serve 5 year terms. The directors are Mike Stonebraker, Kevin Wilkinson, Anastasia Ailamaki, Jignesh Patel and Peter Boncz. The president of the CIDR organization is Michael Stonebraker.
